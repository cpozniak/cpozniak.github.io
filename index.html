Current Situation

Since September 2020, I have been working as an associate professor at Paris 8 university and I have been attached to the Structures Formelles du Langage lab. As a psycholinguist specializing in experimental syntax, my current research focuses on sociolinguistic variation, primarily in French and concerning social gender. More specifically, I work on modeling social properties in linguistic processing.
